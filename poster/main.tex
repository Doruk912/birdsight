  % ── CARD: Introduction ─────────────────────────────────
  \cardlabel{Introduction}
  \begin{tcolorbox}[card]
    \begin{itemize}[leftmargin=1em, itemsep=2pt, topsep=2pt,
                    label=\textcolor{brand}{\textbullet}]
      \item Birds are bioindicators of ecosystem health. Changes in their population may signal environmental issues.
      \item Traditional population monitoring is slow and impossible to scale as it is expert-dependent.
      \item Combining citizen science and automated AI-based identification solves this problem.
      \item BirdSight: a web platform where enthusiasts submit sightings, get instant AI species suggestions, and contribute to biodiversity data collection.
    \end{itemize}
  \end{tcolorbox}

  \vspace{\vgap}

  % ── Bottom: Compact Summary ──────────────────────────────
  {\small
  \textbf{Dataset Scale:} 23 most common species in Turkey with 5000 images each, totaling 115,000 images. Sourced from GBIF$^{[1]}$. Split 80/10/10 for training, validation, and testing.\par
  \vspace{3pt}
  \textbf{Pipeline \& Augmentation:} Images were cleaned, standardized, and augmented extensively during training (cropping, flipping, color jitter, occlusion) to enhance generalization.}
